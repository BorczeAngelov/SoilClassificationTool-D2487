Particle Size Distribution Percentage of Gravel Gravel is the soil fraction with particle sizes larger than 4.75 mm. Percentage of Sand Sand is the soil fraction with particle sizes between 0.075 mm and 4.75 mm. Percentage of Silt Silt is the soil fraction with particle sizes between 0.002 mm and 0.075 mm. Percentage of Clay Clay is the soil fraction with particle sizes smaller than 0.002 mm. Soil Gradation - Option 1 Coefficient of Curvature (Cc) Cc is the ratio of the square of the particle diameter at 30% passing to the product of the particle diameters at 10% and 60% passing. Coefficient of Uniformity (Cu) Cu is the ratio of the particle diameter at 60% passing to the particle diameter at 10% passing. Particle Diameter Percentiles - Option 2 d10 d10 is the particle diameter at 10% passing. d30 d30 is the particle diameter at 30% passing. d60 d60 is the particle diameter at 60% passing. Sieve Analysis Percentage Passing Sieve No.200 This is the percentage of soil particles that pass through a No. 200 sieve (0.075 mm). Atterberg Limits Liquid Limit (LL) LL is the moisture content at which the soil changes from a liquid state to a plastic state. Plasticity Index (PI) PI is the difference between the liquid limit and the plastic limit of the soil. Atterberg Limits Symbol This is the symbol that represents the position of the soil on the plasticity chart. Organic Content Percentage of Organic Content This is the percentage of organic matter in the soil sample.
